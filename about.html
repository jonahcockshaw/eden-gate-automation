Our aim:

Eden Gate Automation offer a comprehensive and quality service for the design and consultation,  installation, repair or maintenance of domestic or commercial electric gates in the South East of England. Our aim is to beat our competitors on both price and quality.

Our background:

We have over 10 years’ experience in the industry. We are a successful and growing family business with outstanding expertise in, and knowledge of, gate automation. All our work is guaranteed and our installations follow strict Health & Safety guidelines.

Want to work with us?

If you are considering securing your property with wooden or metal electric gates or are looking for a reliable repair and maintenance supplier ring 01932 560729  to arrange a free on site consultation. We will come to see you and discuss the alternatives for your installation. We will advise on the best type of gates and operating system to use for your particular property and will show you the different types of access control systems available to you.

fully insured

We have £5,000,000 public liability insurance which gives you the peace of mind that you are dealing with a professional and responsible company.

standards

Our engineers are all CRB checked. All our installations comply with BS/EN standards. We work closely with manufacturers to ensure we offer you the latest products so that all gates are installed to the highest standards of safety, quality and reliability.
